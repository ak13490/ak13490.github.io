05 / Philosophy

How I think about product.

P-01

Structure before speed.

Frame the problem so business, tech, and users align before building.

P-02

Removal beats addition.

The highest-impact changes often remove complexity, not add features.

P-03

First principles, then data.

Decompose to fundamentals; then let measurement — not opinion — decide.

P-04

AI is a lever, not a costume.

Use AI where it changes the product, not to look modern.

P-05

Eval honestly, or you're guessing.

What you choose to measure defines which mistakes you'll miss.

P-06

Ship, then learn.

A working system teaches more than a perfect plan.
